# Growth and Distribution

The plan converts visitors well but says nothing about getting them. A portfolio with no traffic converts no one. This is how people arrive.

## Owned audience first

The YouTube channel @CalebZaudio is the primary channel, not SEO. It is warm traffic that already trusts CalebZ, and the videos double as proof of craft. SEO takes months and starts cold.

| Move | Action |
|------|--------|
| Every video routes to the site | Pinned comment, description link, end screen |
| Site echoes the channel | Embed or link recent breakdowns on the site |
| Video as proof | Before and after walkthroughs are the testimonial substitute |

## Top of funnel

A pure portfolio only ranks for people who already know CalebZ. Educational content captures artists who feel the problem but do not yet know they can hire someone.

- "Why my master sounds quiet on Spotify"
- "How to prep a mix for mastering"
- "Club system versus phone: what translation means"

Each piece gives value first, then positions CalebZ as the obvious hire. Publish as video, repurpose as short text on the site.

## The free master, as a magnet

A free finished master is the strongest acquisition tool for an unknown engineer. It removes all buyer risk and proves quality before any ask. Offer it deliberately, gated lightly (one track, real contact), not as an open firehose.

## Share-link virality

The track-share feature is also distribution. Every private link CalebZ sends a client carries the brand and a soft path back to the site. A clean, fast delivery page makes clients want to share it. Add a subtle "mastered by CalebZ" footer and a link to book.

## Light SEO baseline

Not the main channel, but free wins:
- Title, meta, Open Graph per page. Strong share cards.
- Schema for a local service and person.
- "Mastering engineer, Seattle, electronic" in copy, honestly.
- Fast pages rank better. The perf budget already helps.

## Funnel map

```
YouTube / referral / share link  ->  site  ->  hear A/B  ->  inquiry  ->  paid  ->  delivery link  ->  referral
```

The loop closes when a delivery link brings the next visitor.

## What to measure

- Source of each inquiry (ask in the form or use tagged links).
- Play rate on A/B, the single best intent signal.
- Repeat and referral rate from delivery links.
