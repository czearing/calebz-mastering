# Vision

## Who

CalebZ. Independent electronic music producer and mastering engineer in Seattle.
YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@CalebZaudio

## Goal of the site

Turn a visiting artist into a booked client. Secondary: host private links to deliver mastered tracks.

## Audience

| Segment | Wants | Decides on |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| Independent electronic artists | Loud, clean, release-ready masters | Sound proof, price clarity, trust |
| Labels and managers | Reliable turnaround, consistency | Track record, testimonials |
| Returning clients | A fast way to receive a delivery | Share link that just works |

## Positioning

One line: Mastering for electronic music that competes on any system.

Not a SaaS. Not an AI tool. A craftsman with a portfolio. The site should feel closer to a studio brand than a freelancer page.

## Personality

- Precise, quiet, confident.
- Engineering, not hype.
- Sound first. The work proves itself.

## Voice rules

- Short declaratives. No adjectives stacked three deep.
- No "elevate", "unleash", "sonic journey", "take it to the next level".
- No em dashes. Use periods.
- Numbers over claims. LUFS, formats, turnaround in days.

## Success signals

- Visitor reaches the contact form in one scroll intent.
- Visitor can hear before and after without leaving the page.
- A shared track link opens in under one second and plays instantly.
