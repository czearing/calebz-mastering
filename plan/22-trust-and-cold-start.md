# Trust and Cold Start

At launch there are zero testimonials. A new site must earn trust another way or it converts no one. This solves the cold-start problem until reviews accumulate.

## Substitute trust, in order of power

| Lever | Why it works |
|-------|--------------|
| Hear the difference | The A/B before and after proves skill directly, no claim needed |
| Founder story | A short personal note moves the buyer from company to person and lowers risk |
| Free first master | Proves quality before any payment, removes buyer risk entirely |
| Risk reversal | A revision guarantee removes the fear of paying for a bad result |
| Process transparency | Showing exactly how it works signals competence and control |

## Founder story

Two or three sentences, real, in first person. Not a bio wall. It states who CalebZ is, that mastering is the real work not a side hobby, and why he cares about the sound. Placed near the top, by the hero or just under it.

Draft direction (CalebZ to finalize):
"I am CalebZ, a producer and mastering engineer in Seattle. I master electronic music so it holds up on club systems and phone speakers alike. Send me a track and hear the difference before you decide."

## The free first master

Framing decides whether this raises or lowers perceived value. Never lead with the word free. Frame it as a deliberate proof mechanism with a reason.

- On-site line: "Send me one track. I'll master it so you can hear the result before you pay for anything. One per artist."
- Proof, not desperation. It puts the actual mastering on the line, which is the point.
- Gated lightly: one track, real contact. Not an open firehose.
- See 21-growth for how this also drives acquisition.

## Risk reversal

State it plainly near the booking action:
"Two revisions included. If it is not right, we keep working until it is."
Low risk wording lifts conversion and answers the buyer's main fear.

## Process transparency

The Process section (07, 08) already shows the four steps. Keep it concrete: file prep, reference, private link, revisions. Concrete process reads as a professional, not a gamble.

## How it phases out

As real reviews arrive through 16-reviews, they take over the trust load. The founder story and free master stay, the empty-testimonial risk disappears. Order of trust shifts from earned-by-proof to earned-by-clients over the first months.

## Compliance

Everything here stays within the FTC 2024 rule (19-legal): the free master is not tied to a required positive review, and no testimonial is shown until it is real and verified.
