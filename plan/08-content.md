## Process

Title: How it works

1. Send your mix. WAV, headroom around minus 6 dB, no master bus limiter.
2. I master to your reference and the target loudness for your platforms.
3. You get a private link to hear it in full.
4. Two revisions included. We lock it when you are happy.
