# Pages and Sections

## Routes

| Route | Type | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| `/` | Static | The portfolio, one scroll |
| `/t/[token]` | Server | A shared mastered track |
| `/reviews/new` | Form | Public review submission |
| `/admin/reviews` | Protected | Moderate reviews |
| `/privacy`, `/terms` | Static | Legal docs |
| `/api/inquiry` | Worker | Contact submit |
| `/api/audio/[token]` | Worker | Streamed audio from R2 |

One page does the selling. No blog, no about page sprawl. Depth through sections, not navigation.

## Home flow

| Order | Section | Job | Exit feeling |
|-------|---------|-----|--------------|
| 1 | Hero | State who and what in one breath | I know what this is |
| 2 | Work | Prove the sound, before and after | It sounds better |
| 3 | Services | Tiers, formats, turnaround, price | I know what I get |
| 4 | Process | How a track moves through mastering | This is handled |
| 5 | Testimonials | Social proof from real artists | Others trust him |
| 6 | Contact | Make booking effortless | I can start now |
| 7 | Footer | Links, YouTube, credits | Easy to follow |

## Hero contents

The hero is the signature (see 23-signature): the A/B before and after is the hero, not a headline over a gradient.

- One line: name, role, "Hear the difference".
- The A/B player, above the fold, one toggle, raw mix to finished master.
- Primary action: Book a master. The play is the secondary action, built in.
- Founder story (22-trust) sits just beneath.
- Motif reacts subtly to the playhead.

## Work contents

- 4 to 6 selected tracks.
- Each: title, artist, genre tag, A/B play of before and after.
- Inline player. No modal. No page jump.

## Narrative arc

The page is not a stack of sections. It is one master pass on a single track, in four acts (see 23-signature): raw mix, the work, the proof, the invite. Each section is a beat in that story, so the scroll feels authored.

## Navigation and scent

- Thin fixed header: wordmark left, anchor links (Work, Services, Contact), one Book button right.
- A first-timer must see how to reach Work, Services, and pricing fast. Do not hide them behind scroll alone.
- Anchor scroll to sections. Active section reflected in the header.
- Mobile: header collapses to wordmark and Book; a compact menu reveals the anchors.

## Persistent

- A small Book affordance stays reachable as you scroll so contact is always one intent away.
