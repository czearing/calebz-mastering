# The Signature

Minimalism alone is not memorable. A template can be clean. The site needs one ownable idea that makes CalebZ stick and separates him from LANDR and every other mastering page.

## The hook

Hear the difference.

The A/B before and after is the spine of the site, not a feature buried in a Work section. But the four good ideas (A/B player, shader, type, color) must talk to each other or they read as four widgets. So they are bound into one system.

## The masterwork concept: one track, healed

The whole scroll is one continuous master pass of a single real CalebZ track. Scroll position is the playhead. As the visitor descends, the same song moves from raw mix to finished master, section by section. One song being healed makes every section a beat in a story, so the site reads authored, not assembled.

| Act | Section | State |
|-----|---------|-------|
| 1. Raw | Hero | The mix, unprocessed. Grey, loose, quiet |
| 2. The work | Work, Process | The master forms. Tightening, color arriving |
| 3. The proof | Testimonials, A/B | Full master. Cyan, tight, loud |
| 4. The invite | Contact | The finished result. Book |

## The signature motion moment

A scroll-scrubbed A/B morph: one gesture crossfades waveform, type weight, color grade, and the WebGL surface from before to after, reversibly. Scrubbing up un-masters it. This is the moment a juror remembers. Fade-up staggers are not a moment.

## Where the player lives

| Place | Use |
|-------|-----|
| Hero | "Hear the difference", one labeled toggle, raw to master at the same instant |
| Work | Per-track A/B for the catalog |
| Share page | The same A/B on a private delivery |

## Craft that earns the award

- Gapless, level matched. The only audible change is the master, never volume.
- One thin monochrome waveform, neon cyan playhead. No spectrum bars.
- The toggle has weight: precise easing, tactile state change, BEFORE and AFTER in mono.
- Type and color carry the same before-to-after state as the audio (see 04, 05).

## The line

One repeated phrase across hero, share page, and outreach: Hear the difference. The brand verb.

## Guardrails

- Static prompt invites the first play. Never autoplays.
- Full value without audio: see the deaf-visitor proof in 13.
- Works on mobile, with reduced motion, and with the canvas disabled. The story still reads.
