# Legal and Compliance

Not legal advice. A checklist of what to publish and follow. Confirm specifics with counsel before launch.

## Documents to publish

| Doc | Purpose | Route |
|-----|---------|-------|
| Privacy Policy | What data is collected, why, retention, contact | `/privacy` |
| Terms of Service | Service scope, revisions, refunds, liability | `/terms` |
| Cookie note | Only if analytics or non-essential cookies are used | In privacy |

Link all from the footer.

## Privacy

The contact and review forms collect personal data (name, email, message), so a privacy policy is required under laws like GDPR and CCPA. State:
- What is collected and why.
- How long it is kept.
- That data is used only to reply and is not sold.
- How to request deletion.

Keep collection minimal. Send inquiries by email, store only what a review needs.

## Reviews and testimonials (FTC 2024 rule)

The FTC final rule, in effect since Oct 21 2024, bans fake, AI-generated, and incentivized reviews and undisclosed insider testimonials. Penalty up to $51,744 per violation. Rules for this site:
- Show only genuine reviews from real clients.
- Verify by email before display.
- Never offer payment or perks for a positive review.
- Never write or alter a review to change its meaning.
- Disclose any material connection if an insider is ever quoted.

## Audio rights

- CalebZ masters tracks for clients. Display work only with the artist's permission.
- Get a short written ok to feature a track in the public portfolio.
- Shared delivery links are private: unguessable token, signed audio, optional expiry and password. Pre-release material stays unlisted.
- Do not host or display anything without rights to it.

## Analytics and consent

- Prefer a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool to avoid a consent banner.
- If any non-essential cookie is set, show a consent notice for EU visitors.

## Accessibility as compliance

WCAG 2.2 AA is also a legal floor under the ADA and the EU Accessibility Act (in force June 2025). See 13-accessibility.

## Pre-launch checklist

- Privacy and Terms published and linked.
- Review pipeline verifies authenticity.
- Artist permission on every featured track.
- Share links private and expiring where needed.
- Analytics cookieless or consented.
